# Floor Plan Images Directory

This directory contains floor plan images for the library building.

## Sample Images Needed:
- ground-floor-plan.jpg
- first-floor-plan.jpg  
- second-floor-plan.jpg
- third-floor-plan.jpg
- fourth-floor-plan.jpg

## Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended size: 1200x800px or larger
- Aspect ratio: 3:2 or 4:3
- File size: Under 2MB per image

## Usage:
Upload your floor plan images here and update the Floor model records to reference the correct filenames.

The carousel will automatically display these images when they are available.
